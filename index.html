Product Description A9 SEO · Pre-A+ launch copy
skinside SEOUL Vitamin C Capsule Cream

A vitamin c capsule cream that combines targeted Vitamin C delivery with a full daily moisturizer formula. Ascorbyl Glucoside — a stable, water-soluble form of Vitamin C — is encapsulated and suspended in the gel base, activating when massaged into the skin. This encapsulated vitamin c serum approach helps maintain ingredient freshness throughout the product's use. This approach helps maintain ingredient freshness throughout the product's use.

The formula functions as a niacinamide moisturizer alongside the Vitamin C: Niacinamide and a four-molecule Cica complex (Madecassoside, Madecassic Acid, Asiaticoside, Asiatic Acid) help support an even, comfortable complexion. Four forms of Hyaluronic Acid — Sodium Hyaluronate, Hydrolyzed Hyaluronic Acid, and additional HA variants — deliver moisture at multiple skin depths.

Squalane, Shea Butter, and Jojoba Seed Oil provide nourishing, skin-compatible lipids without heaviness. Panthenol and Vitamin E round out the formula as supporting actives.

The lightweight gel-cream texture absorbs quickly and layers cleanly under sunscreen. Suitable as a vitamin c face cream for morning use, or as an evening moisturizer. Appropriate for all skin types, including combination and oily skin.
